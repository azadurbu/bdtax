About

Duane E. Wermerskirchen

CEO Real Gem Buying Group

Duane has 30 years’experience in retail diamond selling and buying .He started in 1982 with GIA training anda career in buying jewelry from the public and controlled a retail operation for the resale of pre-owned and new jewelry until 2007 when Duane joined a buying firm of 90 locations called GoldStop to supervise the buying and selling of diamonds to wholesale outlets across the world. At present running the Real Gem Buying Group for the main purpose to make the pawn and diamond buying industry better with programs to buy and sell gold and diamonds and to help improve sales and buys.


Gary A. Ross

President Real Gem Buying Group

Gary is a seasoned retailer successful entrepreneur and sales leader. He began his career at Target, then moved on to The Gap and had an 11-year run achieving the position Executive V.P. of Operations, helping them realize record growth and profits. Ross then joinedMusicland where he was Executive V.P. He was the co-founder of their Suncoast Motion Picture brand where he was President. He drove their growth to over 400 stores. After their sale to BestBuy he was recruited to be President and General Manager of a start-up retail beauty and salon strategy called Purebeauty, and guided them in their mission to build-out 50+ stores. Gary then signed on with Blockbuster as President of Emerging Brands developing two startup chains, Movie Trading Co., which grew to 55 stores, and Rhino Video Games, with 65 stores. Ross joined Duane in 2005 to manage operations for their jewelry buying and selling chain with 90 locations called GoldStop.
